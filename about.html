About Lifeline Connect

Lifeline Connect was founded by a group of dedicated medical professionals and patient advocates, driven by a common mission - to connect kidney disease patients with potential living donors. Since our inception, we've facilitated hundreds of successful donor-patient connections, earning recognition and awards for our impactful work.
